Before You Book

The Answers You Deserve

Every question couples ask us before saying yes — answered honestly.

Who is WeddingClickz and what do you do? +

WeddingClickz is a premium wedding photography and cinematic film studio based in Bangalore, India, with active coverage across Mumbai, Delhi, Hyderabad, Goa, Udaipur, Jaipur, Kerala, Dubai and Abu Dhabi. We have captured 1800+ weddings since 2014 and specialise in candid storytelling, 4K cinematic films, pre-wedding shoots and Italian leather albums.

How much does wedding photography cost with WeddingClickz? +

Our wedding photography packages typically start from ₹75,000 for single-day candid coverage and scale up to ₹5,00,000+ for multi-day luxury weddings with cinematic films, drone, albums and destination travel. Final pricing depends on the number of events, locations, crew size and deliverables. Request a custom quote for accurate numbers.

How early should I book my wedding photographer? +

Book 6 to 12 months in advance. Peak Indian wedding season runs October to February, and destination weddings in Udaipur, Goa or Dubai sell out fastest. If your date is within 90 days, contact us immediately — we keep limited last-minute slots open.

Do you travel for destination weddings in India and abroad? +

Yes — we cover destination weddings worldwide. Frequent destinations include Udaipur, Jaipur, Goa, Kerala, Bali, Thailand, Dubai, Abu Dhabi, Italy and the Maldives. Travel, stay and equipment logistics are quoted transparently with no hidden fees.

What is included in a WeddingClickz package? +

A standard package includes candid + traditional photography, a 3–5 minute cinematic 4K highlight film, full event coverage, professionally edited high-resolution images, online private gallery, and an Italian leather wedding album. Add-ons: drone aerial shots, same-day edits, pre-wedding shoots, save-the-date films and second photographers.

How long until I receive my photos and film? +

Edited photos: 4–6 weeks. Cinematic highlight film: 6–8 weeks. Full ceremony film: 10–12 weeks. Printed albums: 12–14 weeks. A teaser reel of 30–60 seconds is delivered within 7 days of the wedding so you can share immediately on social media.

Do you cover Hindu, Muslim, Christian and South Indian weddings? +

Yes — we cover every Indian wedding tradition. Our team is experienced in Hindu, Muslim Nikah, Christian, Sikh, Jain, Tamil, Telugu, Kannada, Malayali, Marwari, Bengali and Punjabi weddings. We understand ritual sequences so we never miss a key moment — pheras, mangalsutra, kanyadaan, sehrabandi, varmala or the saath pheras.

Can I see full wedding albums before booking? +

Absolutely. On request we share 3–5 complete wedding galleries similar to your event type and venue, plus full cinematic films. This is the most accurate way to judge our consistency — anyone can show 20 great frames, but we show you full 800+ image deliveries.

Why should I choose WeddingClickz over other photographers? +

1800+ weddings shot, 4.9/5 rating from 350+ couples, 11+ years in business, full in-house team (no freelancers), and consistent delivery timelines. Every wedding is led by a senior photographer and cinematographer — never assigned to juniors. We carry redundant cameras, lenses and storage on every shoot to guarantee zero data loss.

How do I book WeddingClickz for my wedding? +

Three simple steps: (1) Share your wedding date and event details via WhatsApp +91 97402 22927 or the form above. (2) We send a custom quote within 24 hours. (3) Lock the date with a 25% retainer. The remaining payment is split between the wedding day and final delivery.
