Wedding Photography Cost in India 2025: Complete Pricing Breakdown

By WeddingClickz•Updated 10 May 2026•10 min read

Wedding photography is the one investment from your wedding day that lasts forever. Yet it is also one of the most confusing to budget for — quotes range from ₹15,000 to ₹8,00,000 for the same city, same event type. This guide breaks down exactly what drives that difference.

What Drives the Price Up

  • Number of events covered — mehendi, haldi, sangeet, ceremony, reception each add to cost
  • Crew size — a solo shooter vs. a lead + second photographer + videographer + drone operator
  • Cinematic film — a 4K highlight film adds ₹25,000–₹80,000 depending on length and edit quality
  • Drone — DGCA-compliant aerial coverage typically adds ₹8,000–₹20,000
  • Album — Italian leather flush-mount albums start around ₹18,000
  • Destination travel — flights, hotel, per diem quoted separately and transparently
  • Same-day edits — a teaser reel played at your reception adds ₹15,000–₹25,000

5 Hidden Fees to Watch Out For

  1. Retroactive travel billing — travel and accommodation quoted "separately later" should always be itemised upfront in your contract
  2. Editing ≠ retouching — "editing" sometimes means basic colour correction only. Ask specifically: do you retouch skin, remove blemishes, do background cleanup?
  3. Album excluded — many studios quote photography and mention albums only after booking. Confirm the album is included, and what the page count and print quality are.
  4. Overtime charges — if your reception runs 2 hours longer than planned, are you billed per hour? This should be clearly defined in your contract.
  5. Watermarked delivery — some studios release unwatermarked images only after full final payment is cleared. Understand the payment-delivery sequence.

How to Get Maximum Value

  • Book early — 8–12 months in advance. Last-minute bookings command 20–30% premiums during peak season (October–February).
  • Bundle photography + video — booking both with one studio is significantly cheaper than two separate vendors and produces more cohesive results.
  • Review full galleries — ask for a complete delivery of 400+ images from a wedding similar to yours, not a curated portfolio of 20 highlights.
  • Confirm your assigned photographer — not just the portfolio face. Many studios sell on senior photographer work and assign juniors on the day.
  • Read the contract — a professional contract specifies crew composition, deliverable timelines, image counts, payment schedule and cancellation terms.

Is ₹50,000 enough for good wedding photography in India?

At ₹50,000 you can get a competent candid photographer for a single-day event in most Indian metros. You will likely not get a cinematic film, drone coverage or a printed album. Review full galleries — not highlight shots — before deciding.

Do Bangalore photographers charge less than Mumbai?

Generally yes — Bangalore and Hyderabad are 10–20% more affordable for equivalent quality compared to Mumbai. Destination cities like Udaipur charge a base rate plus transparent travel costs on top.

What is the cheapest reputable wedding photographer option in India?

Mid-level studios in Tier 2 cities with 3–5 years of experience typically offer the best value — around ₹40,000–₹80,000 for good-quality candid coverage with a basic edited gallery. Always verify with full gallery reviews and check that they carry backup equipment.
