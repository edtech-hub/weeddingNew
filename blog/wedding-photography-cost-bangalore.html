Wedding Photography Cost in Bangalore (2026)

By WeddingClickz• Updated 6 May 2026• 9 min read

"How much does a wedding photographer in Bangalore actually cost?" is the single most-asked question we receive. The honest answer is: it depends on what you're paying for. Two studios quoting "₹3 lakh for the wedding" can deliver wildly different products – different team sizes, different deliverables, different post-production quality, different rights to your own images.

This is the most transparent breakdown we can publish without naming competitors. Numbers are based on real 2026 quotes from established Bangalore studios across three tiers, and on what we charge ourselves at WeddingClickz.

The Three Tiers of Wedding Photography in Bangalore

Tier 1 – Entry / Solo Photographer (₹50,000 – ₹1,50,000)

One photographer, sometimes a second shooter, hand-held coverage for a single event (typically the wedding day or reception). You'll usually receive 200–400 edited photos and a short reel. Best suited for intimate weddings of 50–150 guests, registrations, or budget-conscious couples who only need solid documentation.

What's typically not included: drone, cinematic films, premium albums, multi-day events, travel.

Tier 2 – Mid-Range Candid Studio (₹2,00,000 – ₹6,00,000)

This is the largest segment in Bangalore. A team of 4–6 people covers 2–3 events (typically mehendi/sangeet + wedding + reception). You'll receive 600–1,200 edited photos, a 3–5 minute highlight film, a teaser, and often a basic album.

Strong candid coverage, good colour grading, drone usually as a paid add-on. Most Bangalore couples spending ₹30L–₹70L on the overall wedding land here.

Tier 3 – Premium Cinematic Studio (₹8,00,000 – ₹25,00,000+)

A 6–10 person team with cinema-grade cameras, multiple lenses, dedicated drone operator, sound engineer for the film, and a creative director on set. Expect 1,500–3,000 edited photos, 8–15 minute cinematic films, full ceremony films, same-day edits, premium leather albums, social cuts, and a relationship that begins months before the wedding.

This is the tier WeddingClickz operates in, and the tier you'll see at high-budget destination and luxury weddings across India and the UAE.

What Actually Drives the Price?

1. Team size and seniority

A wedding shot by 2 freelancers and one shot by a 9-person studio team are not the same product. The senior creative director's day rate alone is often ₹50,000–₹1,00,000.

2. Equipment grade

A studio shooting on Sony FX cinema cameras, Zeiss CP.3 cinema primes, gimbals and a drone has ₹50–80 lakhs of gear on site. That gets factored in. A studio shooting only on hybrid mirrorless bodies will be cheaper – and usually lighter on the cinematic feel.

3. Post-production days

A premium wedding film is 80–120 hours of editing, colour grading, sound design, and music licensing. A short candid reel is 6–10 hours. The difference shows.

4. Number of events covered

Each additional event (haldi, mehendi, cocktail, sangeet, post-wedding shoot) typically adds ₹40,000–₹1,50,000 depending on hours, team size, and travel.

5. Drone, same-day edits, and special add-ons

Drone work in Bangalore venues requires a permitted operator and adds ₹25,000–₹75,000. A same-day edit (shown live at the reception) typically adds ₹50,000–₹1,50,000.

6. Albums and physical deliverables

An Italian leather, hand-bound 30-page wedding album from a premium studio costs ₹40,000–₹1,20,000 by itself. Hybrid albums, parents' albums, and signature boxes scale from there.

How Much Should You Spend?

Use this rule of thumb: budget 8–15% of your total wedding spend on photography & cinematography combined. Below 5% and you'll feel the gap when you watch the film. Above 18% and you're likely paying for a brand premium that doesn't translate to better images.

  • ₹15–25 lakh wedding → ₹1.5L–₹3L on photography
  • ₹30–60 lakh wedding → ₹3L–₹7L
  • ₹60L–₹1Cr wedding → ₹7L–₹13L
  • ₹1Cr+ luxury wedding → ₹12L–₹25L+

How to Save Without Compromising

  • Cut events, not quality. Cover the wedding and reception fully rather than spreading a small budget across five events.
  • Skip the basic album. Premium studios offer "shoot-only" packages – buy the album later from the same studio.
  • Off-season weekday weddings often unlock a 10–20% discount.
  • Bundle photography + cinematography with one studio – two separate vendors almost always cost more and clash.
  • Avoid "photographer + relative" combos. The Uncle With A DSLR has cost more couples their wedding photos than any other vendor decision.

FAQs

What is the average wedding photography cost in Bangalore in 2026?

The average mid-range wedding in Bangalore spends ₹3L–₹6L on photography and cinematography combined. Premium cinematic studios start at ₹8L and go up to ₹25L+ for luxury multi-day weddings.

How much does a candid wedding photographer cost in Bangalore?

A candid wedding photographer in Bangalore typically charges ₹1.5L–₹4L for a 2-day wedding when booked solo, and ₹3L–₹7L when bundled with cinematography and drone.

Is it cheaper to hire freelancers or a studio?

Freelancers can be 30–40% cheaper but carry higher risk: no backup team, no insurance, and longer post-production delays. Studios cost more but absorb operational risk on the most important day of your life.

Do photographers in Bangalore charge extra for travel?

For weddings within Bangalore city, no. For destination weddings (Mysore, Coorg, Goa, Udaipur), expect ₹40,000–₹1,50,000 in travel and stay charges depending on team size and location.
